## 4. HEURISTICS (Green/Red Flags)
- **Green Flags:** 
    1. **High-Velocity Hiring:** The ability to convince exceptional talent to leave high-salary roles for a seed-stage vision.
    2. **Defensible Network Effects:** A business model where every new user increases the value for existing participants.
    3. **Radical Transparency:** Founders who are "open books" about their metrics and challenges with their team and board.
- **Red Flags:** 
    1. **Static Distribution:** A product that is well-built but lacks a scalable, inherent way to reach customers.
    2. **AI "Wrappers":** AI businesses that lack proprietary data moats, brand lock-in, or structural defensibility.
    3. **Belief in One's Own "B.S.":** A lack of self-awareness that prevents a founder from pivoting when data contradicts their original vision.
